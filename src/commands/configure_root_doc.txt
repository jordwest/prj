================ Choose a root for your projects ==================
Under this directory, your projects will be sorted into directories
based on where their origins are. For example, if you choose a root

    /Users/myhome/src

Your directory structure could look as follows:

    /Users/myhome/src/github.com/jordwest/prj
    /Users/myhome/src/github.com/me/some-project
    /Users/myhome/src/local/scratch
